B A K E D  S C A L L O P S

Baked scallops are a delightful seafood dish where scallops are topped with a flavorful mixture of breadcrumbs, garlic, butter, and Parmesan cheese, then baked until golden and crispy. This preparation highlights the sweet, tender texture of the scallops while adding a rich, savory crust. Often served as an appetizer or a main course, baked scallops offer a delicious blend of textures and flavors that make them a crowd-pleasing choice.



INGREDIENTS



  • 1kg Fresh scallops (shucked and cleaned)
  • 1/2 Cup butter (softened)
  • 4 Cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1/4 Teaspoon salt
  • 2 Cups colby jack cheese (shredded)
  • 1/4 Cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 Cup parsley(chopped finely)
  • Lemon wedges


INSTRUCTIONS



  • 1Make sure you leave one part of the scallop shell after cleaning and shucking the scallops.
  • 2Combine butter, garlic and salt in a small bowl and mix well.
  • 3Assemble scallops on a baking pan.
  • 4Put a small amount of the butter-garlic mixture, cheese, and parsley on each scallop.
  • 5Bake at 350°F or 177°C for 15-20 minutes or until cheese is light brown and bubbly.
  • 6Serve with lemon wedges and hot sauce.
