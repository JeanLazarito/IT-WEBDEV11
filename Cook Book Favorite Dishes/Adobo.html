A D O B O N G  A T A Y

Adobong atay is a Filipino dish made from chicken or pork liver braised in a savory-sour sauce with soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, bay leaves, and black pepper. It often includes onions, potatoes, or bell peppers for added texture and flavor. The result is a tender, well-seasoned dish that balances the rich liver taste with tanginess and saltiness, making it a hearty and delicious addition to any meal.



INGREDIENTS



  • 1 Thumb-sized ginger (chopped)
  • 1 Medium-sized onions (chopped)
  • 4 Cloves of garlic (minced)
  • 600g Chicken liver (washed and strained)
  • 1/2 Teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 Tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1/4 Cup soy sauce
  • 1/3 Cup vinegar
  • 1 Teaspoon sugar
  • Cooking oil for sauteing


INSTRUCTIONS



  • 1Heat oil in a pan. Sauté the chopped ginger until light brown.
  • 2Add the chopped onions and minced garlic. Sauté.
  • 3Add the chicken liver. Sauté until water comes out and becomes light brown. Cook until the water is reduced.
  • 4Add the ground black pepper, oyster sauce and soy sauce. Stir a little until combined.
  • 5Add the vinegar. Cover and simmer for 5 minutes under medium heat.
  • 6Add the sugar and stir gently. Continue cooking until the sauce is reduced.
  • 7Serve with rice and enjoy.
